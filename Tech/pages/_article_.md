title:: <article>

- The article tag identifies a _thing_ that can be independent from other things in a page.
	- ```html
	  <div>
	      <article>
	          <h2>A blog post</h2>
	          <a>Read more</a>
	      </article>
	      <article>
	          <h2>Another blog post</h2>
	          <a>Read more</a>
	      </article>
	  </div>
	  ```
- Inside an `article` tag we should have a title (`h1`-`h6`)
